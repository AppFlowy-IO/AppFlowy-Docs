# ⌨ Shortcuts

## ⌨ Shortcuts

All the available Shortcuts/KeyBindings for [AppFlowy](https://github.com/AppFlowy-IO/AppFlowy) are listed below. You may scroll or use `CTRL` (or `⌘`)`+F` to search for what you are looking for.
